Who We Are

CBS Gaming is composed of a handful of students like yourself working to bring videogames in different shapes and forms to CBS.
We want to see students embrace gaming as a serious industry as well as a playful entertainment platform.
The organization was founded in September 2015 with the same goal.

What We Do

To reach our goal, we organize tournaments with popular games such as FIFA and Counter Strike.
We do LAN-parties and tournaments for the gamers who play the newest games.
For business and networking we visit companies or do product testing of unreleased gaming peripherals!
