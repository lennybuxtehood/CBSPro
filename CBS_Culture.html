Welcome to CBS Culture

Who We Are

CBS Culture is a society for students who are curious and enthusiastic about culture and the world of performing arts.
We aim at creating a community who is keen on exploring the cultural scene of Copenhagen as a group and meeting like-minded
passionate students from all over the city.

What We Do
