Welcome to CBS Improv

Who We Are

We like to think of ourselves as an open, inclusive community, where letting go of control is our core value.
We foster and encourage awkward and spontaneous moments in a creative and liberating environment.
We aim for everyone to leave our workshops feeling uplifted, energised, and special.

What We Do

We meet once a week on Thursdays at 17:30 n the Dalgas Have Building on the CBS Campus.
We will meet in the CBS Move studio in the basement. We usually have an intro level workshop until 19:00
and then a more advanced one until 20:30 - but people of all backgrounds and experiences are welcome!

What is Improv

Improv is a form of theatre in which all elements of a scene or story are made up in the moment.
In our workshops, we coach people to get out of their comfort zone, stay in the moment,
listen and react genuinely, and most importantly, re-learn to say "yes".
